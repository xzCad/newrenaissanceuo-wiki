# Ancient Lich
## Overview
The Ancient Lich is one of the most powerful undead creatures that can be found in Ultima Online: New Renaissance. These ancient masters of necromancy and magic pose an extreme threat to even experienced adventurers. They are among the most dangerous creatures in the realm, possessing vast magical knowledge and devastating spells.
## Statistics
- **Name:** Ancient Lich
- **Hit Points:** 560-595
- **Fame:** 23,000
- **Karma:** -23,000
- **Virtual Armor:** 60
## Combat Stats
- **Strength:** 216-305
- **Dexterity:** 96-115
- **Intelligence:** 966-1045
- **Damage:** 15-27
## Skills
- **Evaluate Intelligence:** 120.1-130.0
- **Magery:** 120.1-130.0
- **Meditation:** 100.1-101.0
- **Magic Resist:** 175.2-200.0
- **Tactics:** 90.1-100.0
- **Wrestling:** 75.1-87.6
## Special Abilities
- **Bleed Immune:** Yes
- **Poison Immune:** Lethal level
- **Unprovokable:** Yes
- **Treasure Map Level:** 5
## Loot
- **Gold:** 800-1000 coins
- **Wands:** 25% chance of random wand
- **Magic Items:** 
  - 31.8% chance of high-end magic item
  - 15.9% chance of exceptional magic item
- **Reagents:** 50-100 reagents
- **Scrolls:** Medium-level magic scrolls (2 packs)
## Spawn Locations
- **Hythloth Dungeon** - Deep levels of the dungeon
- **Covetous Dungeon** - Lower levels  
- **Shame Dungeon** - Deep levels
## Notes
- Ancient Liches are extremely dangerous opponents with massive intelligence and magical power
- They are immune to bleeding effects and lethal poison
- Cannot be provoked by bards due to their unprovokable nature
- They carry the highest level treasure maps (Level 5)
- Their spell repertoire includes the most devastating magic in the game
- Recommended for only the most experienced and well-equipped adventurers
